Tourist Places in Jakarta

National Monument (Monas)

By: Kompas 500 320

The National Monument, also known as Monas, is an iconic landmark representing Indonesia's independence. Visitors can explore the history museum, climb to the top for a panoramic city view, or relax in the surrounding park area.

It's a must-visit for those who want to learn more about Indonesia's journey to independence.

Kota Tua (Old Town)

By: torch.id 670 412

Kota Tua, Jakarta's Old Town, features Dutch colonial architecture, museums, and lively street performances. Visitors can enjoy exploring historical buildings, visiting Fatahillah Museum, and indulging in local cafes.

A perfect spot for history enthusiasts and photographers.

Ancol Dreamland

By: Ancol 820 540

Ancol Dreamland is Jakarta's most famous waterfront recreation area, featuring theme parks, an aquarium, a water park, and beautiful beaches. It's a family-friendly destination with endless entertainment options.

An ideal spot for fun and relaxation with friends and family.
