Local Food from Jakarta

Ketoprak

Ketoprak is a traditional dish from Jakarta made of rice cakes, fried tofu, vermicelli, and bean sprouts, all topped with a savory and sweet peanut sauce. It is usually garnished with fried shallots and crackers, offering a delightful mix of flavors and textures. Ketoprak is a popular and affordable street food, often enjoyed as breakfast or a light meal.

Soto Betawi

Soto Betawi is a traditional Jakarta soup known for its rich, creamy broth made from coconut milk or milk. It typically contains beef or offal, along with potatoes, tomatoes, and a variety of spices. The dish is hearty and flavorful, making it a beloved comfort food in the city.

Kerak Telor

Kerak Telor is a traditional Betawi dish made from glutinous rice, eggs (chicken or duck), and a mixture of shredded coconut and spices. It is cooked over a charcoal fire, creating a crispy and savory crust. Often served during special events, Kerak Telor is a must-try delicacy that reflects Jakarta's rich culinary heritage.
